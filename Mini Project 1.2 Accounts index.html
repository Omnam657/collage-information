Tatyasaheb Kore Institute of Engineering and Technology (TKIET), established in 1983, is an Autonomous college, affiliated to Shivaji University, Kolhapur and approved by AICTE. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in the discipline of Engineering & Technology across various specializations. Candidates can seek admission to the bachelor course if they have a non-zero score in MHT-CET 2022 or a non-zero positive score in JEE Main. Tatyasaheb Kore Institute of Engineering and Technology has tie-ups with several leading corporates of India to provide placement assistance to students. TKIET witnessed 100% placement with highest package of INR 12 LPA.

There are 2500 students enrolled in Accounts.
