Department of Civil Engineering




To promote excellence by providing strong motivation for creative thinking. To inculcate sensitivity towards society and high respect for environment. To trace new career paths with a meaningful interaction with stakeholders. To impart quality technical education with an excellent blend of academic programs and practical knowledge. The department of Civil engineering was established in the year 1983 initially with UG course in ‘Construction Technology’ with 60 intake capacity. Later shifted to UG course in Civil engineering with same intake in the year 1987. At present the department is has a intake capacity of 120 for the first year and 18 for PG in Construction and Management . The department is accredited two times by NBA-AICTE, New Delhi.

There are 2500 students enrolled in Accounts.
