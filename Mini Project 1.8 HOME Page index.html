ABOUT US



TKIET Warananagar offers courses at undergraduate and postgraduate levels. TKIET Warananagar courses are offered in full-time mode. The courses are approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). Tatyasaheb Kore Institute of Engineering and Technology courses are offered in the fields of engineering and technology The institute offers various specialisations in the B.Tech programme. Some of them are:science and engineering, mechanical engineering, civil engineering, chemical engineering and electronics and telecommunication engineering. Similarly, the offered Warananagar PG course specialisations are electronics and telecommunication engineering, mechanical engineering design, chemical engineering and civil engineering construction management.
